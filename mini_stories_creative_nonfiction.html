Spirals

Alone, empty in bed.

The TV that has yet to pick a channel; staring at you, awaiting your next move. The cozy cat that seems to have no worries; to be so innocent, you can not fathom the thought. The salt lamp that dims a moody hue; a heavy weight drifts down as the light glistens. The blue electric guitar; that is now a muted tone with the attachment of the moody hue. A bowl that was once full of cat food, now lies empty as a cozzie cat, is left overfed yet hungry.

The sheets you lay in melt in your fingers as you grasp them for grounding. The phone you can not turn on feels as though it is useless. The cold blue bottle of water; once yours, now awaiting your arrival back. The letters that were once written from you, feel as though they are going to crumble while re-reading your loving words.

The air purifier that resembles white noise, growls as your thoughts attempt to spiral. The TV in your roommates bedroom, mimics happy lives with people laughing and making jokes to one another; rather than discussing issues at hand, issues that need discussion in order for needs to be met. The dog barking downstairs, probably bored since it is stuck inside an apartment all day.

Sweet reminisce of a candle that once was, and a hint of whatever dinner your roommate cooked filled the apartment; smelling as if freshly baked cookies, and an Italian restaurant had a baby.

The kiss that you were left with.

Take me home!
